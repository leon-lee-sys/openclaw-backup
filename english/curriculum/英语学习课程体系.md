## 📚 第1周：Business Meeting（会议英语）

### 学习目标
- 掌握会议常用表达
- 能够主持和参与英文会议
- 理解会议中的商务术语

### 周一：会议开场与主题介绍

**🎧 听力材料：**
> "Good morning, everyone. Thank you for joining today's meeting. The purpose of our gathering is to review Q3 performance and discuss the strategic direction for next year. I've distributed the agenda to all participants. Let's proceed with the first item: quarterly review."

**📝 核心词汇：**
- agenda /əˈdʒendə/ 议程
- proceed /prəˈsiːd/ 进行
- quarterly /ˈkwɔːrtərəli/ 季度的
- strategic /strəˈtiːdʒɪk/ 战略的
- direction /dəˈrekʃn/ 方向

**🗣️ 实用表达：**
- "Thank you for joining today's meeting."
- "The purpose of our gathering is..."
- "Let's proceed to the next item."
- "I'd like to begin with..."

**✍️ 口语练习：** 用英语介绍一个会议的目的和议程

---

### 周二：表达观点与意见

**🎧 听力材料：**
> "I'd like to share my perspective on this matter. From my point of view, we need to consider the long-term implications. Additionally, I believe we should prioritize customer satisfaction over short-term gains. What are your thoughts on this approach?"

**📝 核心词汇：**
- perspective /pərˈspektɪv/ 观点
- implication /ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃn/ 影响
- prioritize /praɪˈɒrətaɪz/ 优先考虑
- satisfaction /ˌsætɪsˈfækʃn/ 满意度
- approach /əˈproʊtʃ/ 方法

**🗣️ 实用表达：**
- "I'd like to share my perspective..."
- "From my point of view..."
- "What are your thoughts on...?"
- "I agree with... because..."
- "I see it differently because..."

**✍️ 口语练习：** 用英语表达对某个商业决策的赞同或反对意见

---

### 周三：打断与澄清

**🎧 听力材料：**
> "Excuse me, could I interject for a moment? I'd like to clarify what you mean by 'cost reduction.' Are we talking about reducing operational costs or cutting staff? Also, could you elaborate on the timeline you mentioned?"

**📝 核心词汇：**
- interject /ˌɪntərˈdʒekt/ 打断
- clarify /ˈklærəfaɪ/ 澄清
- elaborate /ɪˈlæbəreɪt/ 详细说明
- operational /ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənl/ 运营的
- timeline /ˈtaɪmlaɪn/ 时间线

**🗣️ 实用表达：**
- "Excuse me, could I interject...?"
- "I'd like to clarify what you mean by..."
- "Could you elaborate on...?"
- "Just to make sure I understand correctly..."

**✍️ 口语练习：** 模拟会议中打断对方并提出澄清问题

---

### 周四：总结与行动项

**🎧 听力材料：**
> "To summarize what we've discussed today, we've identified three key action items. First, the marketing team will prepare a detailed proposal by next Friday. Second, the finance department needs to conduct a cost-benefit analysis. Third, we'll schedule a follow-up meeting for next month. Does anyone have anything to add?"

**📝 核心词汇：**
- summarize /ˈsʌməraɪz/ 总结
- action item /ˈækʃn ˈaɪtəm/ 行动项
- proposal /prəˈpoʊzl/ 提案
- cost-benefit /kɔːst ˈbenɪfɪt/ 成本效益
- follow-up /ˈfɒləʊ ʌp/ 后续

**🗣️ 实用表达：**
- "To summarize what we've discussed..."
- "We've identified three key action items."
- "Does anyone have anything to add?"
- "Let's review what we've agreed upon."

**✍️ 口语练习：** 用英语总结一次会议的主要内容和行动项

## 📅 第2周：Email Communication（邮件写作）

### 周一：邮件开头与称呼

**📧 邮件范例：**

> Dear Mr. Johnson,
>
> I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to follow up on our conversation last week regarding the quarterly report. Please find attached the requested documents for your review.
>
> Best regards,
> [Your Name]

**📝 核心词汇：**
- attach /əˈtætʃ/ 附件
- follow up /ˈfɒləʊ ʌp/ 跟进
- regarding /rɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/ 关于
- review /rɪˈvjuː/ 审阅
- regards /rɪˈɡɑːrdz/ 问候

**🗣️ 邮件开头常用语：**
- "I hope this email finds you well."
- "Thank you for your prompt response."
- "I am writing to inquire about..."
- "Following up on our conversation..."

**✍️ 练习：** 写一封邮件，向客户询问项目进度

---

### 周二：邮件正文结构

**📧 邮件范例：**

> Dear Ms. Smith,
>
> I am writing to request a meeting to discuss the upcoming product launch. As you may know, our team has completed the market research phase, and we now need your input on the marketing strategy.
>
> Would next Tuesday at 2 PM be convenient for you? Please let me know if you would prefer an alternative time.
>
> Looking forward to your response.
>
> Best,
> [Your Name]

**📝 核心词汇：**
- request /rɪˈkwest/ 请求
- phase /feɪz/ 阶段
- input /ˈɪnput/ 意见
- convenient /kənˈviːniənt/ 方便的
- alternative /ɔːlˈtɜːrnətɪv/ 替代的

**🗣️ 邮件正文常用表达：**
- "I am writing to request..."
- "As you may know..."
- "Would... be convenient for you?"
- "Looking forward to your response."

**✍️ 练习：** 写一封邮件，约客户开会讨论新方案

---

### 周三：邮件结尾与签名

**📧 邮件结尾表达：**

| 正式程度 | 结尾用语 |
|----------|----------|
| 非常正式 | "Yours sincerely," |
| 正式 | "Best regards," |
| 较正式 | "Kind regards," |
| 一般 | "Best," |
| 友好 | "Cheers," |

**📝 核心词汇：**
- sincerely /sɪnˈsɪrli/ 真诚地
- promptly /ˈprɒmptli/ 迅速地
- appreciate /əˈpriːʃieɪt/ 感谢
- assistance /əˈsɪstəns/ 帮助
- cooperation /koʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃn/ 合作

**🗣️ 邮件结尾常用表达：**
- "Thank you for your assistance."
- "I appreciate your time and consideration."
- "Please do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions."
- "I look forward to hearing from you."

**✍️ 练习：** 写一封完整的商务邮件，包括开头、正文、结尾

---

### 周四：邮件回复与转发

**📧 回复邮件范例：**

> Dear Mr. Brown,
>
> Thank you for your email dated March 15th regarding the budget approval.
>
> I have reviewed the attached proposal and find it well-structured. However, I would like to request some additional details on the cost breakdown before I can provide final approval.
>
> Could you please send me the updated figures by end of this week?
>
> Best regards,
> [Your Name]

**📝 核心词汇：**
- approve /əˈpruːv/ 批准
- budget /ˈbʌdʒɪt/ 预算
- breakdown /ˈbreɪkdaʊn/ 明细
- updated /ʌpˈdeɪtɪd/ 更新后的
- figures /ˈfɪɡjərz/ 数字

**🗣️ 回复邮件常用表达：**
- "Thank you for your email dated..."
- "I have reviewed... and find it..."
- "I would like to request additional details on..."
- "Could you please send me... by...?"

**✍️ 练习：** 回复一封关于项目延期的邮件

---

### 周五：本周复习与输出

**📝 本周邮件模板：**

会议邀请邮件：
```
Dear [Name],

I am writing to invite you to a meeting to discuss [topic].

The meeting is scheduled for [date] at [time] via [platform].

Please confirm your availability at your earliest convenience.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

请求信息邮件：
```
Dear [Name],

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to request information regarding [topic].

Specifically, I would like to know [details].

Thank you for your time and assistance.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

**✍️ 本周作业：** 写3封不同目的的商务邮件（会议邀请、请求信息、感谢回复）
